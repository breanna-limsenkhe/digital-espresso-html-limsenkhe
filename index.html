About the Shop

Welcome to Kape Pa Tayo, your cozy neighborhood coffee shop where every cup feels like home. Whether you're starting your morning, taking a study break, or catching up with friends, we’re here to serve freshly brewed coffee, comforting drinks, and light bites in a warm and welcoming space.

At Kape Pa Tayo, we believe coffee is better when shared. So sit back, relax, and let’s enjoy a cup together.
